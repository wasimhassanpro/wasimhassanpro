About

I work at the intersection of government digital transformation, research informatics, and cloud architecture. Finding: Students with visual impairment show statistically significant performance gaps in spatial reasoning tasks (r = 0.55), attributable primarily to instructional design rather than cognitive ability.
This work fills a gap in disability-inclusive education research and informs curriculum design for teachers in Pakistan and South Asia.
